## 1. Purpose

This SLA sets the minimum service standards for beauty and grooming professionals operating on the VARS platform. It is an operational annex to the Vendor Terms and Conditions. Accepting those terms means accepting these standards.

VARS exists because customers trust that the person arriving at their door is verified, professional, and reliable. These standards protect that trust.

## 2. Response Time

You must accept or decline every booking within one hour of receiving it. A reminder is sent at the 30-minute mark.

If you do not respond within one hour, the booking expires automatically. Repeated expirations may be treated as a pattern of non-responsiveness and may result in review.

**Alternative: Auto-Accept.** If you configure an auto-accept zone, bookings from customers within your zone are confirmed instantly. You receive a 5-minute grace window to cancel any auto-accepted booking without penalty. After that window, standard cancellation rules apply.

## 3. Punctuality

You must tap "On My Way" before the gate window closes. The gate window opens up to 2 hours before the scheduled appointment time.

You must arrive at the customer's location within a reasonable timeframe after committing to travel. The customer can track your location in real time while you are en route. Consistent late arrivals (as reflected in customer disputes and reviews) may result in review.

## 4. Service Delivery

You must:

- Deliver the service as described in your listing (name, description, category)
- Complete all services the customer has booked in a single visit
- Mark "Service Rendered" promptly after completing the work. If you do not mark completion, you will receive a reminder 15 minutes after the scheduled service end time. A persistent banner also appears on your jobs screen for any booking past its scheduled end.

Do not leave a booking in "Arrived" status indefinitely. The customer's auto-release timer (2 hours after "Service Rendered") cannot start until you mark the job complete.

## 5. Professionalism

You must:

- Arrive with all tools, products, and equipment needed for the booked services
- Present yourself in a clean and professional manner
- Respect the customer's home, belongings, and personal space
- Clean up your workspace after completing the service
- Communicate politely and professionally at all times

## 6. Cancellation Limits

**Pre-gate cancellations** (before tapping "On My Way"):

- Maximum 2 cancellations per rolling 30-day window before your account is flagged
- At 3 or more cancellations in 30 days, your account is automatically flagged for review
- Flagging may lead to a warning, temporary suspension, or permanent removal at VARS's discretion

**Auto-accept grace cancellations** (within the 5-minute window) do not count toward your cancellation total.

**Post-gate cancellations** (after tapping "On My Way" or after the gate fires): these trigger immediate account restriction regardless of your cancellation count. See Vendor Terms Section 8.2.

## 7. Rating Threshold

Your average rating is calculated from all customer reviews. VARS reserves the right to review and potentially suspend vendors who fall below a sustained average rating of 3.5 stars.

"Sustained" means the average is calculated over your most recent 10 reviewed bookings (or all reviews if fewer than 10). A single bad review will not trigger action. A pattern will.

Vendors approaching the threshold will be notified and given an opportunity to improve before suspension is considered.

## 8. Communication

You must be reachable through the platform during active bookings. This means:

- Keep push notifications enabled while online or while you have active bookings
- Respond to platform notifications promptly (booking requests, reminders, status prompts)

You must not share personal contact details (phone number, email, social media handles) with customers to arrange bookings outside the platform. The platform provides phone number reveal at the appropriate time (15 minutes before the appointment). All other communication should happen through platform channels.

## 9. Hygiene and Safety

You must maintain hygiene standards appropriate for beauty and grooming services performed in customers' homes:

- Use clean, sanitised tools for every appointment
- Dispose of single-use items properly
- Wash or sanitise hands before beginning any service
- Follow standard industry hygiene practices for your service category (e.g. sterilisation for barbing tools, clean brushes for makeup application)

VARS does not prescribe specific hygiene protocols for every service type, but you are expected to meet the professional standards of your trade. Customer complaints about hygiene will be taken seriously and may result in suspension pending review.

## 10. Portfolio and Customer Consent

You may photograph your work for portfolio use only with the customer's explicit consent obtained through the platform's consent flow. The process:

1. Submit a consent request via the app after the booking
2. The customer has 72 hours to approve or decline
3. Approved photos may be published to your portfolio
4. Declined or expired requests mean the photo is not published

Do not photograph customers without their knowledge. Do not upload photos to your portfolio, social media, or any other channel without going through the consent flow for photos involving customers.

## 11. Consequences of SLA Breach

VARS applies graduated enforcement:

**Level 1: Warning.** First instance of a minor breach (e.g. single late arrival, missed "Service Rendered" mark). You receive a notification explaining the issue. No account action.

**Level 2: Temporary suspension.** Repeated minor breaches or a single significant breach (e.g. 3+ cancellations in 30 days, sustained rating below 3.5, hygiene complaint). Your account is temporarily deactivated. You are notified of the reason and what is needed to reactivate.

**Level 3: Permanent removal.** Serious or repeated breaches (e.g. fraud, misuse of customer data, repeated restriction for post-gate cancellation, sustained failure to meet standards after warnings). Your account is permanently deactivated. You are notified in writing.

Post-gate cancellation triggers immediate restriction (not the graduated path above). See Vendor Terms Section 8.2.

VARS retains sole discretion over enforcement decisions. We aim to be fair and transparent, but the platform's integrity and customer safety take priority.

## 12. Metrics Review

VARS reserves the right to review vendor performance metrics periodically, including:

- Average response time to booking requests
- Cancellation rate (rolling 30-day window)
- Average customer rating
- Dispute frequency and outcomes
- Punctuality patterns (inferred from customer feedback and dispute categories)

SLA thresholds (such as the rating floor and cancellation limit) may be adjusted as the platform matures. You will be notified of any changes at least 14 days in advance.

## 13. Review Cycle

This SLA is reviewed:

- Annually by VARS operations
- When platform features change in ways that affect vendor obligations (e.g. new status steps, new cancellation rules)
- When performance data indicates thresholds need adjustment

Changes are communicated via the app or email at least 14 days before they take effect.
