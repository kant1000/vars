## 5. How Booking Works

### 5.1 Selecting Services

You browse vendors by location and service category. On a vendor's profile, you select one or more services, then enter the booking flow.

### 5.2 Scheduling and Access Details

You choose a date and time slot, then provide your access details: building name, floor, flat number, and gate code. These details are shared with your vendor 15 minutes before the appointment, not before. VARS filters access detail inputs to prevent sharing of personal contact information outside the platform.

### 5.3 Card Verification (First Time Only)

If this is your first booking on VARS, you will be asked to complete a one-time card verification step before your booking is created. This is a non-refundable charge of fifty naira (NGN 50) processed by Paystack. It verifies that your card is active and stores a secure payment token so future charges can happen seamlessly. You will see clear disclosure of this charge before it is taken. This step happens once per account. All subsequent bookings skip it.

### 5.4 Booking Confirmation

When you tap "Confirm booking", your booking is created. Your vendor has one hour to accept. No charge is made to your card at this point.

If the vendor does not respond within one hour, the booking expires. No charge is made.

### 5.5 When You Are Charged

Your card is charged only when your vendor commits to travelling to you, not before. This happens when the vendor taps "On My Way" or when the platform detects they are heading to your location. The charge includes the service price (set by the vendor) plus any applicable transport surcharge (calculated by VARS based on the distance between the vendor's operating zone and your location).

The vendor's share of the payment is secured in their Paystack subaccount at the moment of charge. VARS retains a platform fee of 20% of the total. For Pioneer vendors (the first 50 verified professionals on VARS), the platform fee is waived for their first three completed bookings.

### 5.6 Transport Surcharge

If your location is more than 5 km from the vendor's operating zone centre, a distance-based transport surcharge is added to your total. The surcharge is calculated by VARS and shown to you before you confirm. It compensates for the vendor's additional travel time and cost.

## 6. Cancellation

### 6.1 Before Your Vendor Sets Off

You can cancel free of charge at any time before the vendor commits to travel. No charge will have been made, so there is nothing to refund.

### 6.2 After Your Vendor Sets Off

Once your vendor has committed to travelling to you (indicated by the "On My Way" status), the booking is locked. You cannot cancel at this point because the vendor is already en route and a charge has been made. If something has gone wrong, you can raise a dispute (see Section 8).

### 6.3 Vendor Cancellation

If your vendor cancels after committing to travel, you receive a full refund automatically. The vendor is subject to platform penalties.

If your vendor cancels before committing to travel, no charge was made and you are free to book another vendor.

## 7. Service Delivery

### 7.1 Live Tracking

While your vendor is on the way, you can track their location on a map in the app. Location updates every 30 seconds.

### 7.2 Phone Number Reveal

Your phone number is shared with the vendor, and theirs with you, 15 minutes before the scheduled appointment. This is intentional: it connects you at the right moment without exposing contact details unnecessarily early.

### 7.3 Confirming Completion

After the vendor marks the service as rendered, you have two hours to confirm the service is done or raise a dispute. If you take no action within two hours, the payment is released to the vendor automatically.

You can confirm the service is complete at any time by tapping "Confirm service done" on the booking detail screen.

## 8. Disputes

If something goes wrong, you can raise a dispute from the booking detail screen. You will be asked to select a category:

- Vendor didn't show up
- Arrived very late
- Service not completed
- Poor quality
- Wrong service
- Other (free-text required)

When you raise a dispute, the vendor's payment is frozen until a VARS administrator reviews and resolves the issue. VARS aims to resolve all disputes within 24 hours. Possible outcomes:

- Full or partial refund to you
- Payment released to the vendor (if the dispute is not upheld)

Both you and the vendor are notified of the outcome.

If you are not satisfied with the resolution, contact us at hello@bookwithvars.com or lodge a complaint with the Federal Competition and Consumer Protection Commission (FCCPC) or the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) as appropriate.

## 9. Reviews

After a completed booking, you can leave a star rating (1 to 5) and an optional written comment. Reviews are visible to other customers on the vendor's profile.

If you delete your account, your reviews are anonymised to "VARS Customer" but not removed. This preserves the integrity of vendor ratings for other customers.
